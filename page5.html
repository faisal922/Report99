Introduction

 
Our website is a store that cares about selling watches and it allows the customer to bye the watches that what they like. The problem is when the customer has some restrications for example: the customer does not have a transportation or he is physically handicapped we help him to shop in our website easily, so here comes the benefit of our website where you can choose the watches that you want.


Requairement


- Customers can buy watch.
- Customers can add to his cart.
- Customers can delete from his cart. 
- Customers can pay online.
 - Customer can view and check price any watch.  
- Customer can do modification for his cart by modify the quantity of each watch.


System 



Our watch store implemented as a web page that has all the interfaces which allows the user to add and delete for any watch from cart, also it allows the user to modify, view and search  any watch.


Evaluation  



We will expect that The kind of users how open our website are males or females and their ages from 17 to 45, how cares about watches which means that we will create an interface that matches their expectations.


Conclusion
